# A Bit of a Stretch

**Author:** Jacob Van Lunen  
**Date:** April 2, 2009  
**Original URL (Wayback Machine):** <https://web.archive.org/web/20090601202842/https://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/boab/32>

---

Ready to take infinite turns?

Hello, again, and welcome to another edition of Building on a Budget. I spent a good portion of this week trying to decide what I was going to write about. I tried to think of a Standard deck that was offbeat, inexpensive, and had some game, but I just could not think of anything new. I had some friends over this weekend to test Extended, and I decided to throw together a homebrew.

The deck is, at its core, a blue-black control deck. It plays removal spells, various ways to control the board, and a healthy dose of card draw. What makes this deck very different is its late game plan. Once you draw a second [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime), you play a [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix), imprinting [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) and [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch). You can then play the second [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) and take an additional two turns. Every land you draw or have in hand becomes a [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch). Eventually you draw an [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) and use it to put artifact lands on the top of your library. Hence, you have an unlimited number of turns to find a way to win.

The combo here is [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch), [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix), and [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime).

##### The Support Cards

**[Wretched Banquet](https://scryfall.com/card/con/56/wretched-banquet):** This card is very powerful. I'm quite surprised it has not received much play in the first few months of Constructed with *Conflux*. I feel this card has applications from Legacy to Block Constructed. This is a very efficient removal spell and should see more play than it does.

**[Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast):** A very nice removal spell. [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast) is very strong against the Elf and Faerie decks.

**[Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage):** One of the best cards in Extended. This is a very good card and it's a common, so you won't have to break the bank.

**[Thirst for Knowledge](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/79/thirst-for-knowledge) and [Compulsive Research](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/147/compulsive-research):** Drawing cards is important. It's also very important that you have a way to discard your [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch). You don't need to draw one of these to pitch it, though—you can simply cast [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) targeting yourself and pitch the big spell.

##### The [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage) Toolbox

**[Executioner's Capsule](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/92/executioners-capsule):** A good removal spell that can be tutored for with your [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage). Once you have [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) online you can destroy creatures at your leisure.

**[Chalice of the Void](https://scryfall.com/card/a25/222/chalice-of-the-void):** This card is a bit expensive, but it seems like a very nice fit in this deck. I'm only playing one because of the monetary value of the card, but the single copy feels very strong.

**[Engineered Explosives](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/252/engineered-explosives):** Again, I'm only playing one Explosives, I can [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage) for the single copy when it is absolutely necessary and I can use my [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) to reuse it.

**[Sigil of Distinction](https://scryfall.com/card/ala/219/sigil-of-distinction):** Fetching the *Shards of Alara* Equipment with [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage) is a very strong play. My friend Gerard Fabiano recently added a single copy of Sigil to his Extended Faerie deck that plays [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage). From the games I've seen it seems very powerful. This deck needs a way to win the game and this seems like a fine candidate.

I decided that the deck should play a full playset of each of the on-color artifact lands. They make our [Thirst for Knowledge](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/79/thirst-for-knowledge) much better, and they maximize our chances of having one to use with [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) and [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) once we combo.

The deck plays [Arcane Sanctum](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/259/arcane-sanctum) because it fixes our mana and allows us to set an Explosives off for 3, this is surprisingly important. The white mana also gives us more sideboard options.

[Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) is working extra hard here. I wanted to play enough copies of [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) to fight through other blue decks copies. Drawing a second copy isn't the end of the world because it can be easily discarded to your [Compulsive Research](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/147/compulsive-research).

The deck is surprisingly elegant. It doesn't play a single two-drop, so in most matches you want to be setting your [Chalice of the Void](https://scryfall.com/card/a25/222/chalice-of-the-void) at two counters. This isn't new tech; Kenny Öberg played a [Chalice of the Void](https://scryfall.com/card/a25/222/chalice-of-the-void) deck that lacked two-drops and Top 8ed a [Pro Tour](https://web.archive.org/web/20090602011304/https://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/ptber08/welcome) and a [Grand Prix](https://web.archive.org/web/20090602011304/https://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/gphan09/welcome) with it. It won't come as a surprise to most people that Chalice for 2 is very powerful against most decks in Extended.

##### The Sideboard

1 [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast)
 3 [Sunbeam Spellbomb](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/250/sunbeam-spellbomb)
 4 Trickbind
 1 [Night of Souls' Betrayal](https://scryfall.com/card/ima/99/night-of-souls-betrayal)
 3 [Cruel Ultimatum](https://scryfall.com/card/e01/82/cruel-ultimatum)
 1 [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix)
 2 [Cruel Edict](https://scryfall.com/card/dvd/48/cruel-edict)

The fourth [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast) is there for our Elf and Faerie matchups. The [Sunbeam Spellbomb](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/250/sunbeam-spellbomb) is a silver bullet that can be used to gain some much-needed life when racing a Zoo or Burn deck. The Trickbinds are used for fighting the Storm decks. The Night of Soul's Betrayal is, like [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast), used against Faeries and Elves. The [Cruel Ultimatum](https://scryfall.com/card/e01/82/cruel-ultimatum) and extra [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix) give us the option of going all in on our combo. [Cruel Ultimatum](https://scryfall.com/card/e01/82/cruel-ultimatum) is much better than [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch) against Zoo and Burn decks. The Ultimatum also provides us with a very strong win condition.

I played some games with the deck against my friends.

My opponent hasn't seen my deck yet and doesn't know I have [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast). I draw a [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime), play my [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast) killing Wild Nacatl, and pass the turn. My opponent plays Tarmogoyf. I draw another [Seat of the Synod](https://scryfall.com/card/eoc/176/seat-of-the-synod), play [Arcane Sanctum](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/259/arcane-sanctum), and use [Wretched Banquet](https://scryfall.com/card/con/56/wretched-banquet) on the Tarmogoyf. I pass the turn. My opponent plays a third land and plays [Kird Ape](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/137/kird-ape) before passing. I draw a [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch), play my [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage), find [Chalice of the Void](https://scryfall.com/card/a25/222/chalice-of-the-void), and pass. My opponent plays [Lightning Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mkm/218/lightning-helix) targeting me during my end step, then attacks with [Kird Ape](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/137/kird-ape) and passes. On my turn I draw [Compulsive Research](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/147/compulsive-research) and play [Chalice of the Void](https://scryfall.com/card/a25/222/chalice-of-the-void) for 2 before attacking. My opponent [Incinerate](https://scryfall.com/card/jvc/51/incinerate)s me in response to the Chalice, then draws and plays Wild Nacatl before attacking.

I discard [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch) and [Thirst for Knowledge](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/79/thirst-for-knowledge). My opponent draws and plays [Path to Exile](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/141/path-to-exile) on my [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage) and attacks with the 5/4. I take the damage and go to 5.

On my turn I draw another [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch), play [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix), and imprint [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) and [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch). I explain that I can take infinite turns with [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) and [Vault of Whispers](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/322/vault-of-whispers), and my opponent concedes.

**Sideboarding:** I take out the 3 [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast), 3 [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch), and the [Sigil of Distinction](https://scryfall.com/card/ala/219/sigil-of-distinction). My opponent opens up with a [Kird Ape](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/137/kird-ape). I draw another Raven's crime and play my capsule. My opponent attacks for two and plays a Wild Nacatl and [Seal of Fire](https://scryfall.com/card/jvc/50/seal-of-fire). I kill the Wild Nacatl when my opponent attacks. He simply passes the turn back to me. On my turn I draw [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins). I play [Wretched Banquet](https://scryfall.com/card/con/56/wretched-banquet) targeting the Ape and play [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) targeting myself to discard the [Cruel Ultimatum](https://scryfall.com/card/e01/82/cruel-ultimatum), I play one of my [Arcane Sanctum](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/259/arcane-sanctum)s and pass. My opponent see the writing on the wall and concedes the game.

**Matchup overview:** I don't think the Zoo matchup is as good as it seemed. I only had a chance to play two games. The deck is definitely capable of winning the matchup, but the Zoo decks are capable of drawing very well and putting a lot of pressure on us. I'm happy the deck performed well, but I don't want to go out on a limb and say this is a good matchup just yet.

1-0

##### Round 2 vs. Faeries

**Game 1**

I mulligan and keep [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix), [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast), [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime), [Arcane Sanctum](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/259/arcane-sanctum), [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins), and [Vault of Whispers](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/322/vault-of-whispers) on the draw. My opponent opens with an [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island). I draw a [Thirst for Knowledge](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/79/thirst-for-knowledge), play my [Arcane Sanctum](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/259/arcane-sanctum), and pass. My opponent plays a fetchland and passes. I draw a [Swamp](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/291/swamp), play it, and attempt to [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) my opponent. The [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) meets a Spellstutter Sprite, but I have [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast) at the ready. My opponent discards a [Vedalken Shackles](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/218/vedalken-shackles). My opponent plays another land and passes. I dredge my [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast), play my third land, and pass the turn. My opponent misses his fourth land drop. I play [Thirst for Knowledge](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/79/thirst-for-knowledge) on his end step, and he plays Vendilion Clique in response, putting the [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast) in my hand on the bottom. Between the Clique draw and the [Thirst for Knowledge](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/79/thirst-for-knowledge), I draw [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch), [Seat of the Synod](https://scryfall.com/card/eoc/176/seat-of-the-synod), [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage), and [Sigil of Distinction](https://scryfall.com/card/ala/219/sigil-of-distinction). I discard the [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch) and the Sigil. On my turn I draw [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime). I love "Oops, I win!" moments. I play my fourth land, play [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix), imprint [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch) and [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime), and play the [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) in my hand. I show the artifact land, and my opponent concedes.

**Sideboarding:** I take out 4 [Wretched Banquet](https://scryfall.com/card/con/56/wretched-banquet) and bring in [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast), [Night of Souls' Betrayal](https://scryfall.com/card/ima/99/night-of-souls-betrayal), and 2 Trickbind. The Trickbind can be used to counter my opponent's [Ancestral Vision](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/144/ancestral-vision)s.

**Game 2**

I keep an opener of [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast), [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime), [Swamp](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/291/swamp), [Vault of Whispers](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/322/vault-of-whispers), [Seat of the Synod](https://scryfall.com/card/eoc/176/seat-of-the-synod), [Arcane Sanctum](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/259/arcane-sanctum), and [Arcane Sanctum](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/259/arcane-sanctum). My opponent mulligans to four and plays a Mutavault. I draw an [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island), play a [Swamp](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/291/swamp), and use [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime). My opponent discards a Vendilion Clique, then plays a second land and attacks for 2 with Mutavault. On my turn I draw [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage) and play [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) twice. My opponent discards a [Cryptic Command](https://scryfall.com/card/ima/48/cryptic-command) and a Venser. My opponent attacks with his Mutavault again and passes. On my turn I draw another land and play [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage) finding my [Sigil of Distinction](https://scryfall.com/card/ala/219/sigil-of-distinction). My opponent draws a third land, plays it, and attacks me again with Mutavault. On my turn I use [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) on my opponent, who discards [Spell Snare](https://scryfall.com/card/ecl/71/spell-snare). I use [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) until my opponent has no cards left in hand and then attack with my [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage). My opponent draws and attacks again. On my turn I draw yet another land, play my Sigil for 4, make my [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage) a 5/5, and attack. **Match overview:** This seems like a good matchup. We have all the tools we need to win an attrition war. They have to counter our [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix), but we have [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) and can try to resolve [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) every turn until they run out of counters.

##### Round 3 vs. Astral Slide

**Game 1**

On my turn I play another land and use [Thirst for Knowledge](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/79/thirst-for-knowledge). I draw [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch) and two lands. I discard [Wretched Banquet](https://scryfall.com/card/con/56/wretched-banquet) and [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch) before passing. My opponent cycles some lands and finds a [Lightning Rift](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/129/lightning-rift), which he plays before passing the turn. I draw another [Compulsive Research](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/147/compulsive-research) and play it, drawing another [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) and a pair of [Wretched Banquet](https://scryfall.com/card/con/56/wretched-banquet). I discard the [Wretched Banquet](https://scryfall.com/card/con/56/wretched-banquet)s. My opponent plays a land and starts Rifting me with lands. He passes the turn, and I play the [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix) and put six extra turns on the stack. I proceed to draw three [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast) in a row. The next turn I draw a [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage), the Mage finds a land, and I put two more turns on the stack. On my seventh extra turn I draw [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) and use it to put an artifact land on the top of my library. My opponent concedes.

**Sideboarding:** I take out 3 [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast) and 4 [Wretched Banquet](https://scryfall.com/card/con/56/wretched-banquet). I bring in one [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix), three Trickbind, and three [Sunbeam Spellbomb](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/250/sunbeam-spellbomb). The Trickbinds aren't that great here, but they're significantly better than our [Darkblast](https://scryfall.com/card/rav/82/darkblast), the [Sunbeam Spellbomb](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/250/sunbeam-spellbomb)s are used as cantrips to maximize our chances of drawing the combo.

**Game 2**

I keep [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island), [Swamp](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/291/swamp), [Arcane Sanctum](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/259/arcane-sanctum), [Compulsive Research](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/147/compulsive-research), [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch), [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix), [Thirst for Knowledge](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/79/thirst-for-knowledge). My opponent plays a land and passes. I draw a [Chalice of the Void](https://scryfall.com/card/a25/222/chalice-of-the-void), play a land, and pass. The game basically functions as draw go. My opponent plays lands and cycles them and starts gaining card advantage with [Life from the Loam](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/96/life-from-the-loam). Eventually he plays a Kitchen finks on his ninth turn. I still haven't found a second Raven's crime, but my opponent isn't putting much pressure on me. I suspect he sided out most of his win conditions to stop my combo. He has a few [Ancient Grudge](https://scryfall.com/card/tsr/151/ancient-grudge)s in his graveyard that he has dredged up and I can't go off just yet. I'm at eleven when I draw my tenth land and handcast my [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch). I have a pair of [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix) in my hand but I only have one [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) in my graveyard. I draw another [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch) and play it. It's really frustrating when you take four extra turns and aren't sure if you can win. On my fourth turn I draw the second copy of [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) and decide I have to go for it. I play my [Chalice of the Void](https://scryfall.com/card/a25/222/chalice-of-the-void) for 2 to stop his [Ancient Grudge](https://scryfall.com/card/tsr/151/ancient-grudge)s. I then play my [Spellweaver Helix](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/247/spellweaver-helix) and imprint the combo. I play the [Raven's Crime](https://scryfall.com/card/mma/95/ravens-crime) but my opponent has a [Ghost Quarter](https://scryfall.com/card/cm2/253/ghost-quarter) that he can use on my [Academy Ruins](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/309/academy-ruins) when I draw it. I decide to just use every land I draw as a [Time Stretch](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/69/time-stretch) and try to get there. I get to a [Trinket Mage](https://scryfall.com/card/ddu/41/trinket-mage) and find my Sigil. I play my Sigil for 10 and equip it the next turn, but it gets Slided out. This goes on for awhile until my opponent runs out of cyclers. I'm lucky enough to have drawn a few card-drawing spells and I have six extra turns on the stack. Eventually I'm able to attack, and I draw enough card draw and land to keep my opponent out of the game.

**Match overview:** This match seems very easy. Be careful for Duergar Hedge-Mage after sideboards. It's best to hold artifact lands to use with the combo if you are able. Try fetching your Chalice and playing it for 2 on the turn you combo—you need to protect your Helix from [Ancient Grudge](https://scryfall.com/card/tsr/151/ancient-grudge). If your opponent has a Hedge-Mage in play with Slide, you need to save a Trickbind and the mana to use it—otherwise they can break up your combo. This may seem like a tall order, but their deck is very slow and you have plenty of time to set up a big turn.
