# Building On A Budget - Gunner

**Author:** Nate Heiss  
**Date:** January 19, 2004  
**Original URL (Wayback Machine):** <https://web.archive.org/web/20090601202842/https://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/magiconline/articlenh011904>

---

[![Orcish Artillery](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/10e/220?format=image&version=normal)](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/220/orcish-artillery)

Way back in the stone age of **Magic**--when [Erhnam Djinn](https://scryfall.com/card/mb2/133/erhnam-djinn)s battled with Swords to Plowshares--players dueled not with netdecks and finely honed skills, but rather with whatever they had in their bag and a little bit of luck. People won with style, and they had fun doing it.

Those of you who remember those days undoubtedly remember a little gunner named [Orcish Artillery](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/220/orcish-artillery). Orcish Artillery is a cool card; it shoots your opponents, their creatures, you…pretty much anything it can fire at. Orcs just like shooting things I guess.

Some creative person read the card a few times and decided to use our little gunner in a deck that included [Spirit Link](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/29/spirit-link). This didn't stop the Artillery from shooting everything in sight, but now instead of a wild child running amok in a house of glass, the Artillery resembled a ward patient bouncing around his padded cell--causing lots of chaos, but not hurting himself.

That deck was a ton of fun to play, yet I haven't seen many people using it in the past few years. Well, this deck is about crazy Orcs, Goblins, gaining life, and overkill. You might not win the Pro Tour with this, but you're going to have a blast. A "hit you for 7 and gain 7" kind of blast.

**Building on a Budget - Gunner**

- 12 Mountain
- 8 Plains
  *(20 lands)*

- 4 Deftblade Elite
- 4 Goblin Grappler
- 4 Goblin Striker
- 4 Lava Hounds
- 4 Orcish Artillery
- 4 Sparksmith
  *(24 creatures)*

- 4 Bonesplitter
- 4 Loxodon Warhammer
- 4 Shock
- 4 Spirit Link
  *(16 other spells)*

*(Full decklist with sideboard: `decklist.txt`; priced breakdown: `decklist_priced.md`)*

Yep--all the platinum hits. Cards that will just make you smile when you slam them down onto the table. [Goblin Grappler](https://scryfall.com/card/lgn/100/goblin-grappler), [Lava Hounds](https://scryfall.com/card/8ed/198/lava-hounds), [Goblin Striker](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/94/goblin-striker), [Orcish Artillery](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/220/orcish-artillery)--they're all here. And they're just itching to be equipped with [Bonesplitter](https://scryfall.com/card/cmr/458/bonesplitter)s and Warhammers...and then getting [Spirit Link](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/29/spirit-link)ed; taming their reckless ways.

The basic plan is to either get an [Orcish Artillery](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/220/orcish-artillery) or [Sparksmith](https://scryfall.com/card/ons/235/sparksmith) into play with [Spirit Link](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/29/spirit-link) or [Loxodon Warhammer](https://scryfall.com/card/c21/249/loxodon-warhammer) on it. Sometimes you'll get interesting things going with your little creatures like [Deftblade Elite](https://scryfall.com/card/plst/LGN-12/deftblade-elite), [Goblin Striker](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/94/goblin-striker), and [Goblin Grappler](https://scryfall.com/card/lgn/100/goblin-grappler). [Lava Hounds](https://scryfall.com/card/8ed/198/lava-hounds) is your backup fatty when you need to get some real meat into the red zone. The joys of tapping a Sparksmith to deal 4 damage to a creature and netting 4 life is hard to convey with the written word. And, let me assure you, you'll have a smile on your face the whole time your Goblin Striker is swinging in with a Loxodon Warhammer in its fist.

You have to be careful about over-committing. Usually there aren't any problems, but sometimes your whole board will implode to a cycled [Slice and Dice](https://scryfall.com/card/cmm/255/slice-and-dice). Be careful--running out of creatures on the board to equip is no fun. You'll generally use your mana to equip and not to play all your creatures--doing so impacts the game while conserving cards in your hand. The worst feeling in the world is when you have equipment in play and a [Spirit Link](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/29/spirit-link) in hand but nothing to put them on. Take care this doesn't happen to you.

##### Tips On Playing The Deck

[![Loxodon Warhammer](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c21/249?format=image&version=normal)](https://scryfall.com/card/c21/249/loxodon-warhammer)

- Sometimes you need to [Spirit Link](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/29/spirit-link) your [Lava Hounds](https://scryfall.com/card/8ed/198/lava-hounds). That's fine; however, it's rarely correct to Spirit Link a 1/1 unless it's [Sparksmith](https://scryfall.com/card/ons/235/sparksmith).
- Don't be afraid to use [Spirit Link](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/29/spirit-link) creature carrying a [Loxodon Warhammer](https://scryfall.com/card/c21/249/loxodon-warhammer)--you'll just gain double the life!
- [Deftblade Elite](https://scryfall.com/card/plst/LGN-12/deftblade-elite) is often best used on defense. (And, yes, its ability will keep you from gaining life off of it.)
- Try to keep your [Bonesplitter](https://scryfall.com/card/cmr/458/bonesplitter)s on the same creatures attacking with the Warhammer so you net more life.
- Remember that you can pass the Warhammer around. Shooting with a Warhammered [Sparksmith](https://scryfall.com/card/ons/235/sparksmith) and then passing it to an attacker is powerful and will gain you a ton of life.
- Don't be afraid of trading your Grapplers away for creatures unless you need them as Goblin fuel for [Sparksmith](https://scryfall.com/card/ons/235/sparksmith).
- [Orcish Artillery](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/220/orcish-artillery) is a 1/3--don't forget it makes a great blocker!

##### Adding Money To The Deck

[![War Elemental](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/mrd/112?format=image&version=normal)](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/112/war-elemental)

The coolest card would be Exalted Angel with a Warhammer or [Spirit Link](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/29/spirit-link) for some really insane life-gaining action. If you want to go for something cheaper, try [Essence Sliver](https://scryfall.com/card/tsb/8/essence-sliver) and then maybe add a few [Hunter Sliver](https://scryfall.com/card/lgn/102/hunter-sliver)s to mix things up.

If you're looking for something really bizarre, try out [War Elemental](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/112/war-elemental). You should be able to sneak it out there after shooting your opponent with [Orcish Artillery](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/220/orcish-artillery), then attacking with your other creatures to start making it big.

If you're going to add Exalted Angel and/or [War Elemental](https://scryfall.com/card/mrd/112/war-elemental), I recommend adding [City of Brass](https://scryfall.com/card/tmc/62/city-of-brass) as well.

You could also upgrade the [Lava Hounds](https://scryfall.com/card/8ed/198/lava-hounds) into [Siege-Gang Commander](https://scryfall.com/card/plst/DMR-136/siege-gang-commander)s. That should put a boost in your [Sparksmith](https://scryfall.com/card/ons/235/sparksmith)'s ability. The only other real cool addition to the deck might be a [Goblin Sharpshooter](https://scryfall.com/card/c13/111/goblin-sharpshooter). Not because it's great in the deck, but rather because it makes a great gunner, and fits right in with the theme.

Until next time, sling that fire.
