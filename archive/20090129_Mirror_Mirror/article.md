# Mirror Mirror

**Author:** Jacob Van Lunen  
**Date:** January 29, 2009  
**Original URL (Wayback Machine):** <https://web.archive.org/web/20090601202842/https://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/boab/23>

---

It's been a year since the *Lorwyn* Prerelease. I remember the tournament well, though. I opened a card that got me really excited.

[![Mirror Entity](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/otc/83?format=image&version=normal)](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity)

Very little explanation is needed for those of you that had the pleasure of playing with [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) in Limited. The card outright wins games. It demanded that your opponent have a removal spell right away. I expected this card to be a runaway Constructed hit. Unfortunately, it never really caught on. I still can't figure out why this card hasn't seen much play. In honor of my favorite Lorwyn rare ... may I humbly present "Mirror Mirror."

##### How It Works

Overall, it's a pretty straightforward concept. However, there are a few hidden gems here. You can use [Heritage Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/171/heritage-druid) to protect the [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) and his minions the turn you play them:

Pay {1} to activate [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity). All my creatures are now 1/1 Elves. Tap six creatures for six mana and activate [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) again to make all my creatures 6/6.

Each card plays a particular role in the deck's inner workings.

[Treefolk Harbinger](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/239/treefolk-harbinger): [Treefolk Harbinger](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/239/treefolk-harbinger) fetches up our [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) and holds the fort while the deck amasses an army. It's important that we actually draw a [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) or [Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane). The harbinger is one of the best ways to do this.

[Llanowar Elves](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/227/llanowar-elves): [Llanowar Elves](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/227/llanowar-elves) ramp us into our powerful token producers like [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger) and [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession). Once [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) gets going, any size body becomes an extremely relevant threat.

[Heritage Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/171/heritage-druid): [Heritage Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/171/heritage-druid) is very important in protecting the [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity). We can make huge amounts of tokens and a [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) and just get our key card burned out. [Heritage Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/171/heritage-druid) lets us use the "gain all creature types" clause on [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) to protect it from whatever one of our opponent's might play.

[Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid): [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid) ramps our mana very quickly. We can play this fine young lady on our second turn and use it to play a [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger) on our third. It can also be used to combo with other cards in our deck. We can activate our [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) for an arbitrarily large amount and use our [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid) to play whatever our hearts desire.

[Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity): Not much needs to be said for this card. The deck is built around abusing the current lack of Wrath effects in the casual room and nothing takes advantage of that more than [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity).

[Hunting Triad](https://scryfall.com/card/cma/116/hunting-triad): Our deck wants to be playing a lot of token production. [Hunting Triad](https://scryfall.com/card/cma/116/hunting-triad) doubles as a combat trick.

[Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession): Three fliers for one card. This seems absolutely amazing to me.

[Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger): The best of all the token producers in the deck. [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger) is an amazing threat on its own and can become especially absurd when you're combining it with a [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) or [Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane).

Activate Ajani, swing with my team, before blockers are declared; tap three tokens give Cloadgoat Ranger +2/+0 and flying.

[Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane): [Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane) is absurdly powerful. He makes your attacks more potent and makes your opponent's attacks very difficult.

[Crib Swap](https://scryfall.com/card/ecl/11/crib-swap): A removal spell for problem cards. You can search for it with your Harbinger.

The deck seemed pretty tight, so I brought it into the casual room to play some games.

##### Round 1 vs. Elementals

I mulliganed down to six on the play and kept [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid), [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger), [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger), and [Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane). My [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid) was the first play of the game. I had drawn into a pair of [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains). My opponent played a "comes into play tapped" land on his first two turns. I played my turn-three [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger) and passed. My opponent played a [Ravenous Rats](https://scryfall.com/card/gvl/37/ravenous-rats). I discarded a [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains). I played [Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane) and attacked with the team. I gave my [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger) +2/+0 and flying before blockers were declared. On my opponent's turn he played [Firespout](https://scryfall.com/card/clu/190/firespout) destroying everything but my [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger). I played another [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger), used Ajani's -1 ability and got in for 7 in the air with the first Ranger (I used the tokens from the other to give the first flying.) My opponent scooped it up the next turn.

1-0

##### Round 2 vs. Red Burn

I kept [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Llanowar Elves](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/227/llanowar-elves), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity), [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), and [Hunting Triad](https://scryfall.com/card/cma/116/hunting-triad) on the draw. My opponent played a [Ghitu Encampment](https://scryfall.com/card/ddn/33/ghitu-encampment) and I played my Elves. My opponent had a second-turn [Incinerate](https://scryfall.com/card/jvc/51/incinerate) for my Elves. I played a second land and played the Heritage druid I had drawn. My opponent played a Boggart Ram-Gang and attacked for 3. I drew [Crib Swap](https://scryfall.com/card/ecl/11/crib-swap), played a land and passed. My opponent attacked me and I played the Swap. My opponent passed. I played [Hunting Triad](https://scryfall.com/card/cma/116/hunting-triad), and my opponent played [Flame Javelin](https://scryfall.com/card/plst/DD2-53/flame-javelin) targeting me. On his turn he played Demigod of Revenge. I went to 8. On my turn I played [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession) and Devoted druid. He attacked with the Demigod and I blocked with a token. I played [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) the next turn. He attempted to [Incinerate](https://scryfall.com/card/jvc/51/incinerate) the Entity but I activated it for 4. On his turn he played another Demigod and attacked with both. I untapped my devoted druid and activated my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity). I tapped six untapped "Elves" and activated the [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) for 6. I blocked both Demigods. The next turn I was able to swing for lethal damage.

2-0

##### Round 3 vs. a black deck with [Underworld Dreams](https://scryfall.com/card/thb/121/underworld-dreams)

I kept [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid), [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger), and [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) on the draw. My opponent played a [Swamp](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/291/swamp) and passed. I drew a [Llanowar Elves](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/227/llanowar-elves), played it, and passed. My opponent played another swamp and [Terror](https://scryfall.com/card/dmr/103/terror)ed my Elves. I played [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid) and passed, and my opponent played a turn-three [Underworld Dreams](https://scryfall.com/card/thb/121/underworld-dreams). I untapped, drew Crib [Swamp](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/291/swamp), and played [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger). On his turn he played another [Underworld Dreams](https://scryfall.com/card/thb/121/underworld-dreams). I played [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) and attacked with my team, making everything a 2/2. On his turn he untapped and played a [Cruel Edict](https://scryfall.com/card/dvd/48/cruel-edict), then another. I sacrificed two tokens. On my next turn I activated my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) for 5 and attacked to end the game.

3-0

##### Round 4 vs. White Control

I mulliganed on the play and kept [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession), [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession), and [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession). My opponent and I played [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains) back and forth at each other until I played a third and played [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession). On his turn he played a [Story Circle](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/49/story-circle). I ripped like a champ and drew [Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane) to attack for 6. He played another [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains) and passed. Wary of [Wrath of God](https://scryfall.com/card/cmm/70/wrath-of-god), I attacked for 6 (which he prevented) and gained 2 life with Ajani. I continued to attack and keep my opponent tapped out preventing the damage until I had amassed a pair of [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger)s in my hand. I played a [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger) and gave my team some counters, and my opponent once again prevented the damage from the tokens. On his turn he played [Wrath of God](https://scryfall.com/card/cmm/70/wrath-of-god). On my turn I played another [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger) and a [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession) and gave my team some counters. My opponent untapped, played a land, and passed. I gave my team some more counters and attacked, and my opponent prevented most of the damage. On his next turn he played an [Austere Command](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/121/austere-command) to destroy all creatures. He slowly used his [Story Circle](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/49/story-circle) to prevent me from doing anything. Eventually he played a [Twilight Shepherd](https://scryfall.com/card/dvd/11/twilight-shepherd) and attacked me until I died.

3-1

##### Round 5 vs. Red Creatures

I mulliganed on the play and kept [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Treefolk Harbinger](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/239/treefolk-harbinger), [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession), and [Hunting Triad](https://scryfall.com/card/cma/116/hunting-triad). I played the Harbinger and fetched my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity). My opponent played a [Mountain](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/858/mountain) and a Goblin Mountaineer. I untapped, drew another [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession), played another land, and passed. He played Mudbrawler Cohort and attacked with both creatures. I blocked the 2/2. I drew a [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid) and played it. He attacked with both again, and I blocked both. He tapped out for Ashenmoor Gouger. I untapped, drew a [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), and played [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession). He attacked with everything again, and I blocked all except the Gouger. I drew [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger) and played it. On his turn he attacked with just the Ashenmoor Gouger. I took the 4, then he played another Gouger. On my turn I drew a [Heritage Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/171/heritage-druid), played the [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) and the Druid, and attacked for some damage in the air. He attacked with both Gougers, and I blocked one with my Harbinger. He played [Spiteful Visions](https://scryfall.com/card/dsc/233/spiteful-visions). On my turn I drew a land and Ajani and played them both, gave my team some counters, and attacked with everyone. After blocks, I activated my mirror entity for 1 and then used all my creatures to make more mana with [Heritage Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/171/heritage-druid). All my guys ended up being 8/8s, and I won that turn.

4-1

##### Round 6 vs. Mono-Blue Control

I was on the play, and I kept [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid), [Hunting Triad](https://scryfall.com/card/cma/116/hunting-triad), [Treefolk Harbinger](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/239/treefolk-harbinger), and [Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane). I played my Harbinger on turn one and got my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity). My opponent played an [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island) and passed. I drew a [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains) and played my [Devoted Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ecc/104/devoted-druid), and my opponent played another [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island) and passed. I played [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession), and it resolved. He played another [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island). I drew [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger), attacked for 3, and attempted to play the Ranger, but my opponent had [Remove Soul](https://scryfall.com/card/8ed/95/remove-soul). He played another [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island). I drew another Ranger, attacked for 3, and my second Ranger met another [Remove Soul](https://scryfall.com/card/8ed/95/remove-soul). He played another [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island) and passed again. I attacked with my team, and he flashed in a Faerie Harbinger searching up Faerie Trickery. After blocks, I used my [Hunting Triad](https://scryfall.com/card/cma/116/hunting-triad) to pump the token. I then used the Druid and my leftover [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains) to play my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity). He drew, played an [Island](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/289/island), and passed. I attacked with everyone and used [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) to make some nice pumps, and he conceded.

5-1

##### Round 7 vs. Red-Green Aggro

On the draw, I kept [Llanowar Elves](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/227/llanowar-elves), [Heritage Druid](https://scryfall.com/card/ema/171/heritage-druid), Trefolk Harbinger, [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), and [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger). My opponent played [Llanowar Elves](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/227/llanowar-elves) on turn one, and I mirrored the play. On turn two he played [Troll Ascetic](https://scryfall.com/card/cm2/146/troll-ascetic) and passed. I played my Harbinger, getting another [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity), and played my Druid. My opponent played a [Blanchwood Armor](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/213/blanchwood-armor) on his Troll (with two [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest)s in play), attacked me for 5, and passed. I drew and played my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity). On his turn he played another [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Incinerate](https://scryfall.com/card/jvc/51/incinerate)d my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity), and attacked for 6. On my turn I played another [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity). He played a [Puncture Blast](https://scryfall.com/card/eve/60/puncture-blast) this time and attacked again. I drew the land I needed and played a [Cloudgoat Ranger](https://scryfall.com/card/khc/21/cloudgoat-ranger). He attacked with the Troll, and I chump-blocked it with the Harbinger. I drew [Hunting Triad](https://scryfall.com/card/cma/116/hunting-triad) and attacked with everyone but two tokens. (That's enough to survive one burn spell. If he has two burn spells I'm dead anyway.) I used the Triad to pump my Ranger and put my opponent on a two-turn clock. He played a sixth land and attacked. I tried to chump-block with a token, but he had Epic Proportions for the win.

5-2

##### Round 8 vs. White Weenie

I was on the play, and I kept [Llanowar Elves](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/227/llanowar-elves), [Treefolk Harbinger](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/239/treefolk-harbinger), [Ajani Goldmane](https://scryfall.com/card/m11/1/ajani-goldmane), [Forest](https://scryfall.com/card/msh/295/forest), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), [Plains](https://scryfall.com/card/msc/866/plains), and [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession). I played my Elves on the first turn, and my opponent played [Goldmeadow Harrier](https://scryfall.com/card/ddf/3/goldmeadow-harrier). I played [Treefolk Harbinger](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/239/treefolk-harbinger), fetching my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity), on turn two. He played a [Wizened Cenn](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/49/wizened-cenn). I played [Spectral Procession](https://scryfall.com/card/mm2/33/spectral-procession) on turn three, and he played [Kithkin Harbinger](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/26/kithkin-harbinger) fetching up another [Wizened Cenn](https://scryfall.com/card/lrw/49/wizened-cenn). I played Ajani and gave my team some counters before sending in the flyers. On his turn he played the Cenn and attacked with the Harbinger. I blocked it with my Harbinger, and everyone lived. On my turn I played another land and played the [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity), gave my team more counters, and attacked with the flyers again. He untapped and played his own Ajani, killing both of them. I untapped, activated my [Mirror Entity](https://scryfall.com/card/otc/83/mirror-entity) for a lot, and attacked, but he stopped me with [Holy Day](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/21/holy-day) and attacked back On my next turn I activated and attacked for just enough to kill him, and met another [Holy Day](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/21/holy-day). Things went on like this until he had played all four [Holy Day](https://scryfall.com/card/10e/21/holy-day). Eventually he didn't have a Fog, and I won the game.

6-2
