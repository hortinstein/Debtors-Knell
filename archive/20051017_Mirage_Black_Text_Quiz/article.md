Today's **Magic** Arcana takes a trip in the way-back machine to the *Mirage* era of the color black. How many black cards can you name from their rules text alone? To make it a bit more challenging, we'll only provide you with the text boxes from the cards' *playtest* versions, so their wording may not represent exactly what ended up on the card. (To protect the cards' anonymity, any references to the card itself have been blurred out.) Happy guessing!
